About Antique Adda

For over three decades, Antique Adda has been the premier destination for discerning collectors and enthusiasts seeking authentic, museum-quality antiques. Our passion lies in preserving history — one exquisite piece at a time.

Every item in our curated collection is hand-selected by our team of expert appraisers, ensuring authenticity, provenance, and exceptional craftsmanship. From rare Victorian furniture to ornate Art Deco jewelry, we bring the world's finest antiques to your doorstep.
